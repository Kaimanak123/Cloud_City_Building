About Cloud City Building

Built in Glasgow.
Built to last.

We started Cloud City Building in 2022 with one idea: a building company should be the easy part of a renovation, not the hard part.

Our Story

One team, every trade

Most renovation projects fall apart between trades — the joiner waiting on the plumber, the plasterer waiting on the electrician, nobody quite in charge. Cloud City Building was set up to fix that, by bringing joinery, plumbing, gas engineering, electrical work, plastering, brick laying and tiling into a single, coordinated team.

Since registering in 2022, we've worked across Glasgow's residential and commercial properties, taking on everything from single-room refurbishments to full extensions — always with one point of contact, one quote, and one standard of work.

Why It Works

No gaps, no guesswork

Because every trade sits under one company, there's no finger-pointing if something needs adjusting mid-project, and no delay waiting for a separate contractor to become available. We plan the order of works ourselves, so your job moves at a proper pace from start to finish.
